排版

排版是门手艺活。美印简历采用学术界广泛应用的 TeX 排版引擎，提供精益求精的排版质量：

  • 美观匀称的版面控制
  • 精确细致的分页断行
  • PDF 输出，跨平台，保证 100% 一致的打印和显示效果

for a hyphen, type a hyphen (-);

for an en-dash, type two hyphens (--);

for an em-dash, type three hyphens (---);

for a minus sign, type a hyphen in mathematics mode ($-$).

Donald Knuth in The TeXBook

字体

字是门面。美印简历精心挑选搭配中英文字体。一叶飘落而知秋，细微之处见真章。

  • 中英混排，不同文字，不同字体
  • 标点挤压，妥善缩减连续标点及行首／行尾标点的多余空间
  • 字偶距调整，合字替换，启用高级字体特性，精雕细琢

The term kerning also takes on a different meaning. In digital typesetting, kerning is a second advance vector that is added to the first.

Yannis Haralambous in Fonts and Encodings

  • 多套色彩搭配，简约精致

A grid breaks space or time into regular units. A grid can be simple or complex, specific or generic, tightly defined or loosely interpreted. Typographic grids are all about control.
